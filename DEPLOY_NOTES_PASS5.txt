Deploy:
1. Open this zip.
2. Copy the CONTENTS of GLITCHED-MATRIX-Prototype-Lab-main into the root of your GitHub Pages repo.
3. Commit/push all changed files, especially index.html, style.css, admin.js, assets/js/demos.js, and assets/data/*.json.
4. Wait for GitHub Pages to finish deploying.
5. Hard-refresh once. If your browser still shows old media, clear site data for glitchedmatr1x.github.io or open in a private window.
